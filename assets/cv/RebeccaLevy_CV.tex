\section*{\large Research Interests \vhrulefill{1.0pt}}
\vspace{-6pt}

I investigate
\vspace{-6pt}
\begin{itemize}
\itemsep-6pt
\item The role of stellar feedback in shaping the interstellar medium and galaxy evolution
\item Young, embedded star clusters, especially in the centers of nearby galaxies
\item Starburst galaxies, especially NGC\,253 and M\,82
\item Multiphase gas kinematics and dynamics in nearby galaxies
\end{itemize}
\vspace{-6pt}
using multiwavelength photometric and spectroscopic data from ground- and space-based observatories\\
{\small (e.g., radio; (sub-)millimeter interferometric; near-, mid-, far-infrared; optical IFU)}
